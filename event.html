'15 LINEUP

Droppin' Deuce

Christina's brothers, naturally, perform every year. Brian on the drums. Jason on the Bass. They are Droppin' Deuce.

Hunter's Cannon

Hunter's Cannon calls Philadelphia and its suburbs home. They burst onto the Philly music scene like a shotgun blast, playing several weekly gigs for their first two years. Legend holds that the band has only had two "official" practices; they prefer to work out their songs live.

HighKick

HighKick is a West Philadelphia based band who continue to evolve and challenge themselves as performers. Presenting their fans with improvisational jams, layered indie sagas, bluegrass singalongs and a trilogy of Christmas Musicals to boot. HighKick aim to please and shoot to kill.

The Griz Band

Affectionately known by all who love him as Griz (and currently performing under that pseudonym), singer, songwriter and multi-instrumentalist, Chris Grunwald, is a maverick in the Philadelphia music community. Loosely described as an Americana-Folk artist, his work defies an easy pigeonhole as it effortlessly pushes the boundaries of compatible genres including reggae, rock, alternative and country, while still maintaining his unique and recognizable signature sounds.

Forty Ounces

Forty Ounces is Philadelphia’s only Sublime tribute act that keeps true to spirit of Sublime. The parties, the atmosphere and the loyal fans make the shows unique and chilled out like the music and Long Beach California itself. Sublime created a style of music and a life style that has been embraced by people all around the world but it was tragically cut short. Forty Ounces is proud to be the only Philadelphia based Sublime tribute bringing you the music, the show and the fun that is Sublime.
