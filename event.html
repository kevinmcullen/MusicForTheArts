EVENT

Our annual event affords artists the opportunity to donate or just display their work at the benefit. It's a great opportunity to network and get your art out there for people to see.

If you are interested in participating or donating, please contact Jason Cullen at admin@musicforthearts.com. (You may need to bring supports/tables/etc. to hang your work.)
